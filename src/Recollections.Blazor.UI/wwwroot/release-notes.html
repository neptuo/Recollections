New features

  • New Highest altitude view, with dedicated versions on beings and user profiles
  • Map, stories, and highest altitude views on user profiles
  • Swipe between months on the calendar view
  • Tap entries on calendar and map views to see a quick card preview
  • Push notifications when your connections share new entries
  • Daily "On this day" push notifications for anniversaries of your own and shared entries
  • Global drag-and-drop upload form with media preview before upload
  • Upload progress visible on entry media, with a lighter progress visual in the PWA
  • Manage GPX tracks on entries and see total elevation
  • Loading progress bars for async operations across the app
