Prague Trip 2025 2025-06-15 · 08:30

Morning walk through Old Town

Old Town Square, Prague · 50.0870°N, 14.4209°E

Woke up early to catch the sunrise over Charles Bridge. The light was absolutely magical, painting the Vltava river in shades of gold and amber. At 6 AM, the bridge was almost empty — just a few photographers and a lone street musician playing violin.

Walked through the narrow cobblestone streets toward Old Town Square. The Astronomical Clock was about to strike the hour, and a small crowd had already gathered.

Stopped at a tiny bakery for a coffee and koláče — the pastry was still warm from the oven. Sat on a bench and just watched the city wake up. These quiet morning moments are my favorite part of traveling.
